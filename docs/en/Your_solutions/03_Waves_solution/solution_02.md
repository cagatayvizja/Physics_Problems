3. The Solution: Calculating Wave Speed (v)
Now, we use the universal wave equation, which links speed, frequency, and wavelength:

v=f×λ
Plugging in our values:

f=330 Hz

λ=1.28 m

v=330×1.28
v=422.4 m/s
